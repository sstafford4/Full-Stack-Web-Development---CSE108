Volume 1

Denji is a young man crushed by debt, inherited from his late father. To survive, he works as a devil hunter for the yakuza, killing devils using his pet devil, Pochita, who has chainsaw powers. One day, the yakuza betray Denji, and he is killed by a zombie devil. However, Pochita sacrifices himself to save Denji, merging with his heart and reviving him as "Chainsaw Man" — a human-devil hybrid with chainsaw abilities.

Volume 2

Denji begins his new life as a Public Safety Devil Hunter under Makima’s supervision. He’s introduced to Aki Hayakawa, a more experienced devil hunter, and Power, a fiend (a devil who has taken over a human corpse) with an impulsive personality. Denji, Aki, and Power are assigned to work together, though their relationship is initially tense.

Volume 3

The team faces off against the powerful Leech Devil after defeating the Bat Devil, further proving the dangers of Denji’s new life. Makima continues to manipulate Denji’s feelings, subtly controlling him with promises of affection and advancement if he performs well. Denji becomes more entrenched in his devil-hunting role, facing more dangerous missions and testing the limits of his powers.

Volume 4

The team is assigned to a hotel to track down a devil connected to the Gun Devil. They find themselves trapped by the Eternity Devil, a seemingly invincible enemy that creates a looping space in which the team is stuck. Tensions rise as the group faces the psychological toll of being trapped, with some members, particularly Kobeni, panicking.
