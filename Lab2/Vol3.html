Volume 9

The aftermath of the battle with Santa Claus leaves Denji and his team reeling. However, the focus soon shifts to Aki, whose time is running out due to his contract with the Future Devil. Aki, haunted by visions of his and Denji’s deaths, becomes increasingly desperate as he edges closer to confronting the Gun Devil.

Volume 10

Makima’s manipulations reach their peak as she reveals her true identity: the Control Devil. She has been orchestrating many of the events in Denji’s life, controlling not only devils but humans as well. Makima’s ultimate goal is to break Denji’s spirit and claim the Chainsaw Devil’s heart for her own purposes, using it to bring about her version of world peace by erasing the concept of death, war, and suffering.

Volume 11

Determined to defeat Makima, Denji confronts her in a final showdown. Using his wits and newfound resolve, he manages to outsmart her, delivering a fatal blow in a shocking and unconventional manner. Instead of relying on brute strength, Denji embraces the humanity that he had almost lost.

Volume 12

The beginning of Part 2 of Chainsaw Man, introducing a brand new co-protagonist, Asa Mitaka!
