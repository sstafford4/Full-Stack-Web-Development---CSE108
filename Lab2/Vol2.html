Volume 5

The Gun Devil's looming threat grows, and Denji continues to rise in the ranks of Public Safety after his victory over the Eternity Devil. Aki's desire for revenge against the Gun Devil intensifies, and his contract with the cursed sword devil takes a heavier toll on his life expectancy. Meanwhile, Denji's bond with Power strengthens, as does his fascination with Makima.

Volume 6

Denji is heartbroken by Reze’s betrayal but is forced to confront her in a brutal fight. Reze’s powers as the Bomb Devil prove incredibly dangerous, and their battle wreaks havoc across the city. Denji, with the help of his allies, fights for survival. Despite the violence, Reze’s feelings for Denji seem conflicted, and she offers him a chance to run away with her.

Volume 7

Following Reze’s defeat, Denji returns to his devil-hunting duties, though his emotional scars remain. The Public Safety Division faces new threats as a series of deadly assassins from around the world are sent to target Denji. These include the terrifying Santa Claus, a mysterious figure with the power to control multiple doll-like bodies and spread chaos.

Volume 8

The conflict with Santa Claus reaches its climax as Denji and his team face the true extent of Santa's horrifying powers. Santa’s dolls flood the city, turning people into mindless, murderous puppets. Denji is forced into a deadly showdown in hell, facing off against powerful devils and Santa Claus herself. The battle is intense, pushing Denji to his limits and forcing him to tap into even more of his chainsaw powers to survive
